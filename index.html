About Bao Blinds

Monsoon Blinds

Bao Blinds, a prominent player in the blinds manufacturing industry, has been a stalwart since its establishment in 2007. Specializing in both indoor and outdoor blinds, the company has solidified its position as a leading manufacturer known for producing high-quality window coverings. With corporate offices strategically located in key cities such as Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Coimbatore, Bao Blinds has successfully catered to the diverse needs of its clientele across these regions.

Advantages of Monsoon Blinds

Wind Protection

Monsoon Blinds are designed to withstand strong winds, acting as a barrier to prevent debris and dust from entering your balcony space.

Rain Protection

With Monsoon Blinds, you don’t have to worry about unexpected rain showers. They are waterproof and designed to keep your space dry and comfortable.

UV Protection

Protect your balcony furniture, cushions, and other accessories from harmful UV rays. Our Monsoon Blinds offer long-lasting UV protection to preserve your items.
